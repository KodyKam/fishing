Important Requirements

Mandatory

Fishing License

Required by Ontario Law

Affordable: $30-$50 for residents
Fines: $100s for unlicensed fishing
Options: 1-year or 3-year license
No license = No fishing. Get yours before the trip!

Complete Trip Checklist

Mandatory Items
  • Valid Fishing License
  • Government-approved PFD/Life Jacket
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Sturdy footwear (no flip-flops)
  • Photo ID
Recommended Items
  • Sunglasses & Hat
  • Sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • Bug spray
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Portable phone charger
